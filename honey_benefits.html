Honey properties

Honeys have low pH value, high sugar content, and hydrogen peroxide in them. Some honeys such as Manuka honey have a special compound called Methylglyoxal that help with its antibacterial properties. There are many other phenolic acids and flavonoids found in honey that characterize them as well.

Case Studies of Humans & Honey

According to this scientific article, honey from clover, basswood, citrus, thyme, tea plant, Howers, buckwheat, eucalyptus, Labiatae, and Manuka has beneficial effects on certain parameters, such as cardiovascular risk factors, satiety, glucose tolerance, mucositis symptoms in cancer patients, URTIs symptoms in children, wound healing.
